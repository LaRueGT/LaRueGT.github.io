Introduction

I'm an ambitious nerd with a big personality. I tend to attack my problems head on and prefer brutal honesty over comforting stories.

I'm concerned about our digital rights, particularly with software patents and net neutrality, but I am weary of the constant efforts to encroach upon them. I also care a lot about the challenges men in my generation are facing, to me there is too little concern among the population for many troubling issues facing guys.

Where I'm From

I was born in California but moved to North Carolina at a young age. I consider myself from a small southern town even though I never picked up the accent.
